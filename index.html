About Us

Founded in 2016, United We Stand is a visionary organization forged by a group of impassioned individuals united by a common goal — advocating for immigration rights and fostering a more inclusive society. Born from the firsthand experiences and shared values of our founders, our organization is a testament to the belief that everyone deserves equal opportunities, regardless of their background or place of origin.
